Business Case

Pragma

Stop ECOA violations and EU AI Act breaches before they execute. Pre-execution blocking, immutable audit trail, compliance certificates — built for fintech and enterprise AI teams.

The Problem

AI is making credit, hiring, and healthcare decisions at scale — with no automated guardrails against the specific regulations that carry real fines.

Regulatory Liability

ECOA, EEOC, GDPR, and the EU AI Act carry fines up to €35M or 7% of global revenue. The CFPB and EEOC are actively pursuing AI discrimination cases — Workday's class action covers millions of applicants.

Monitoring Is Too Late

Every compliance tool on the market monitors after the fact. By the time a report flags a biased loan denial, the harm has happened and the liability is already created.

Proxy Variables Are Invisible

Zip code, last name, and email domain are race and national origin proxies under ECOA. Models use them routinely. No existing tool detects them in real-time and blocks the decision before it executes.

Value Proposition

Pre-execution blocking, not post-hoc reporting. Every AI decision is screened against specific regulations before it executes.

Use Cases

Real scenarios where the engine detects risk and provides actionable guidance.

Hiring Decision

Input decision:

"Reject job candidate" + context: gender=female, education=non-elite

Output:

bias flagged fairness flagged

Recommendation: Remove gender from evaluation criteria. Apply structured scoring on skills and experience only.

Loan Approval

Input decision:

"Reject loan application" + context: zip_code=90210, income=75000

Output:

discrimination flagged bias flagged

Recommendation: Zip code is a proxy for race and protected class. Replace with income, credit score, and debt-to-income ratio.

Promotion Decision

Input decision:

"Promote employee" + context: performance=excellent, years=3, gender=male

Output:

bias detected merit criteria present

Recommendation: Proceed, but remove gender from criteria. Decision appears merit-based — verify against team-wide promotion rates.

AI Policy Rollout

Input decision:

"Implement AI performance monitoring" + context: scope=keystrokes, consent=not required

Output:

transparency flagged harm potential

Recommendation: Require explicit employee consent. Disclose scope fully. Implement opt-out process and data retention limits.
